❓ Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How can I apply for admission? ⬇
You can apply online through the university's admission portal or visit the admission office.
❓ What programs does the university offer? ⬇
We offer undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines, including Engineering, Business, and Arts.
❓ How do I access my class schedule? ⬇
You can log in to the university portal and check your timetable under "My Schedule."
❓ Who do I contact for technical support? ⬇
You can reach out to the IT help desk via email at support@university.edu.
